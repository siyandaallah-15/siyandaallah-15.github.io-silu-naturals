About Us
Rooted in Nature.
Built for Growth.

SiLu Naturals is a South African hair care brand based in Port Elizabeth, Eastern Cape. We were created to meet the need for high-quality natural products that promote hair growth and a healthy scalp for everyone.

We manufacture and supply natural hair care products while offering a network marketing business opportunity for independent sales agents to resell the products and earn a 100% profit plus ongoing commissions.

Currently available across South Africa.

Vision

To become the most trusted and recommended natural hair care brand in the world.

Mission

Manufacture the best natural hair care products while empowering people with a direct selling financial opportunity.

100% Profit

Buy at R100, sell at R200 — you keep the full profit on every product you sell personally.

Incentives

Earn promotional gifts and vacation rewards as you grow your rank and team.

Commission Structure

As you grow your team and sales volume, you advance in rank and earn higher commission percentages. All products are sold at R200. Your cost price is R100 — so every personal sale gives you R100 profit plus your commission on team sales.
